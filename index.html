What We Do

The Rutgers Entrepreneurial Society hosts a variety of events to support young entrepreneurs and connect them to the wider entreprenurial community.

Click on an event to learn more.

  • Member Meetups

    Fun and interactive bi-weekly meetings hosted at the C.A.S.T.L.E, where we play entrepreneurship-based games, discuss leading events in the news about entrepreneurship, and network with other members to discuss new ventures.

  • Speaker Series

    Lectures and discussion sessions lead by top leaders in entrepreneurship, creativity and innovation designed to expand perspectives, change mindsets and empower student entrepreneurs.

  • Workshops

    Engaging seminars and hands-on activities covering a variety of topics such as How to Make a Business Plan, Market Validation, eCommerce and SEO, Design Tools, and Marketing Strategy.

  • Field Trips

    Trips to neighboring business meccas (NYC, Philly, etc) to attend meetups, conferences, tour startups and co-working spaces, and more.

Be around great ideas and creative people.

Coworking and Community Space

At our coworking space, the CASTLE, you can find members working on projects, practicing pitches, and socializing.

The CASTLE (formerly Lipman Learning House) is the large white house located next to the Lipman Hall bus stop on the Cook/Douglass Campus.
