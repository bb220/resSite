What We Do

The Rutgers Entrepreneurial Society hosts a variety of events to support young entrepreneurs and connect them to the wider entreprenurial community.

Click on an event to learn more.

  • Member Meetups

    Bi-weekly gatherings hosted at our co-working space where members get together to discuss the latest news, brainstorm ideas, share knowledge, and play entrepreneurship-based games.

  • Workshops

    Engaging seminars and hands-on activities covering a variety of topics such as How to Make a Business Plan, Market Validation, eCommerce and SEO, Design Tools, and Marketing Strategy.

  • Co-Working Space

    Newly renovated and located on the Cook/Douglass campus, the CASTLE is where we work on our projects, network with others, practice pitching ideas, and even work on school assignments. Be around great ideas and creative people.

    The CASTLE (formerly Lipman Learning House) is the large white house located next to the Lipman Hall bus stop.
