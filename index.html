What We Do

The Rutgers Entrepreneurial Society hosts events and activities to support and encourage the rapidly growing entrepreneurial scene. Click on an event to learn more.

Member Meetups

Bi-weekly gatherings hosted at our co-working space, the C.A.S.T.L.E. Our members get together to discuss the latest news, brainstorm ideas, share expertise, and play entrepreneurship-based games.

Workshops

Engaging seminars, lectures, and workshops that cover a variety of topics related to entrepreneurship. Previous workshops have covered business plans, market validation, eCommerce and SEO, basic coding skills, design, and marketing.

Co-Working Space

The C.A.S.T.L.E is our newly renovated co-working space on the Cook/Douglass campus where RES members work on their projects, network with others, practice pitching their ideas, and work on school assignments. Be around great ideas and creative people. It is the large white house located right next to the Lipman Hall bus stop.
